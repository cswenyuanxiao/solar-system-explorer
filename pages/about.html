About & Team

Solar System Explorer is a learning-focused web app that visualizes planetary data and integrates NASA APIs for exploration.

Mission

Make space education accessible with clean design, performance, and multilingual support.

Principles

  • Performance-first
  • Accessibility
  • Internationalization
  • Progressive enhancement

Team

Independent developer and collaborators. Contributions welcome via GitHub.
