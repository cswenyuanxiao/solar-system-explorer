Venus

Earth's Twin - The Hottest Planet

The Morning Star

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and Earth's closest planetary neighbor. It's often called Earth's twin due to similar size, but its extreme greenhouse effect makes it the hottest planet in our solar system.
